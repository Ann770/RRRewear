How It Works

RRRewear is your gateway to a sustainable, stylish, and community-driven fashion ecosystem. We make clothing swaps easy, eco-conscious, and enjoyable—because fashion can be both fabulous and ethical.

1

Choose What You Love

Browse a wide range of clothing items shared by other users. If you like something, send a swap request to the owner. Once both users agree to the swap, the process begins!

2

Send Your Clothes

Package your clothing and ship it to the RRRewear headquarters using our pre-paid label. A small shipping fee of just £2.99 will apply.

3

We Handle the Rest

Once your clothing arrives, we ensure it's sent to the respective user you swapped with—completely free of charge! You'll soon receive the item you loved, making the whole process seamless and rewarding.
